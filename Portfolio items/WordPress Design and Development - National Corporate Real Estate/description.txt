Project description
RED Development is one of America's top corporate real estate companies. A luxury marketing agency asked Wavepoint to join them as a design + development partner to create a website ecosystem for RED worthy of a significant marketing spend. ✅ Top to bottom corporate website redesign
✅ Custom maps to display a complex library of properties
✅ Property landing pages searchable by keyword and location
✅ Bold design leveraging video, vibrant colors, and clean typography
✅ White label agency partnership
✅ WordPress multisite
✅ Implementation of technical SEO requirements
