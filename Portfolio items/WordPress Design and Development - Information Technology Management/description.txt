Project description
ConductorIT was launching its IT management business with a small portfolio of clients in Southern California. They needed a website that conveyed their expertise while projecting a "large trusted company" image. I designed and built them a showcase that made their clients feel their firm could be trusted to manage the demands of modern technology. Project features included:

✅ Unified set of related on-brand video backgrounds
✅ UX optimized to drive lead inquiries
✅ Extensive use of detailed, unique icons to help communicate a wide range of services
✅ Designed to project a new business as a seasoned player
