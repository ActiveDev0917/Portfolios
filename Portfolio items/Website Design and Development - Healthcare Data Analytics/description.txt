Project description
❍ Challenge
A pharmaceutical and biotech data analytics company needed a complete redesign and redevelopment of their website to better establish their brand credibility, explain their services, and entice employee recruits.

❑ Solution
The new site's design visually captures the level of professionalism reflected in the company's team operations while creating an aspirational look and feel that appeals to gifted professionals who are the ideal target employees.

✰ Outcome
The client feels their core mission and company culture are accurately reflected in their website, and they report greater returns on recruiting efforts and a greater overall initial understanding of the company's opportunities.
