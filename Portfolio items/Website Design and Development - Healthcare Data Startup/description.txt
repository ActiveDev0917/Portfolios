Project description
❍ Challenge
A healthcare analytics startup revolutionizing mobile data capture needed a website that explained their mission, simplified the presentation of their technology offering, and established them as a trustworthy data partner.

❑ Solution
Expert use of the brand's custom abstract graphics and icons helped create an online environment that conveys the gravitas required for the topic of medical data while communicating an overall approachability and ease of use.

✰ Outcome
The venture's website announces its academic credentials and current studies, invites new users to explore the product offering, and simultaneously launches a parallel podcast that showcases product demonstrations and successes.
